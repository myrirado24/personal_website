Overview

Purpose

Cooking was something really important in my family and as I grow up I started to cook for myself. The more and more that I cooked I started to save recipes from my family, friends, the internet, and cookbooks. The more and more I collect recipes the more unorganized became they were all over the place, so one day as I was reorganizing my recipes I thought to myself: "It would be great if there was a way that I can organize my recipes in a better way"? with that in mind, I came up with the idea I create a recipe organizer that would be simple and easy to alter if needed. This would be great for people that love cooking and want to be organized.

Audience

Plate Pal's audiance are those who would like to take an interese on cooking or beaking. Keeping track of the many recipes that you cooked can be stressful and this tools will make it easier for those who like a more organized cookbook.

Content

Home page

The reason why people will come to my website will be the convenient and the simple way to organize recipes. With my experience I have many recipes that I have in many different places, in paper in my drawers, on my notes on my phone, and bookmarked on my search engine. There are a lot of places and I lose them all the time. This will fix that by having them all in one place. The home page will be an introduction to the user to what they can expect from my website. It will have a visual to and a brief bullet point on the website, and at the end it will have a few Q&A and reviews from current users for the new users.

Images for the Home page

Recipe book

This subpage is where the user will be able to add their recipes to their recipe book. On this page, the user will have the option to manually add the recipe, or if they want, they can directly link the recipe from another website to their recipe book. If the user wants to add the recipe manual there will be a lot of editor options like the title, ingredients section, and directions. Meal Prep

For my second subpage, this will be where you can plan what recipes you would like to cook for the day, week, or month. For those who like to plan, this will be a very efficient way to plan it out. First you pick the recipes that you would like to cook, the next step will be to organize it on the calendar for either the day, week, or month.
